## Architecture quick-reference

**Stack:** SvelteKit + TypeScript + `@sveltejs/adapter-cloudflare`, Tailwind, Vitest 4, Zod v4, Svelte 5 runes mode.

**Verdict pipeline** (`src/lib/verdict/`):
- Pure function `computeVerdict({ date, today, species, launch, data }) → Verdict`
- Four-layer fail-stop: Legal → Safety → Quality → Logistics
- Layer 1 fail → NO-GO. Layer 2 fail → NO-GO. Layer 2 incomplete → INCOMPLETE. ≥2 Layer 2 warns → CONDITIONAL with bailout.
- `dataSources` tracks which data sources contributed to each Verdict (live/missing/not-applicable per source). Renders as a chip on the DayCard.

**Safety routing** (`runSafety.ts`):
- Open-ocean launches with live buoy on today's date → buoy branch (swell + period + alignment + wind from NWS point)
- Open-ocean today with no buoy → INCOMPLETE (the "May 17 case" guard — never silently fall back to forecast)
- Everything else → NWS zone forecast (CWF text product, parsed prose) with NWS point forecast layered for wind
- Lagoons get a `Spit status` advisory check when `hasOceanFacingSpit: true`

**Data sources** (`src/lib/fetchers/`):
- `ndbc.ts` — NDBC buoy 46244 (Humboldt Bay, wave-only) + 46022 (Eel River, has wind)
- `nws-zone.ts` — NWS CWF text product for PZZ450 (fetched via product endpoint, NOT the deprecated structured forecast endpoint)
- `nws-point.ts` — NWS point forecast for launch coordinates
- `tides.ts` — NOAA tides station 9418767 (Humboldt Bay North Spit)
- `currents.ts` — NOAA tidal currents HUB0203 (slough + bay only)
- `suntimes.ts` — local compute via `suncalc` (civil dawn/sunrise/sunset/civil dusk)

**Privacy posture:** all personal identifiers scrubbed from public repo working tree and git history. Brand fingerprints removed from public-facing docs but the user's private gear inventory is generic in `reference/context.md`. See `DISCLAIMER.md` for the "not for general use" framing.

## Launches (6)

All launches in `src/lib/config/launches.ts` mirror profiles documented in `reference/launches.md`.

### Trinidad Harbor
- **Profile:** Open ocean, requires swell + period + alignment + wind checks. Solo not permitted in year 1 (logistics-layer informational note). No bar to cross. NDBC 46244 primary, NDBC 46022 offshore reference.
- **Buoy gate:** Today's verdict at Trinidad REQUIRES live NDBC 46244 — no forecast fallback. INCOMPLETE if buoy dark.
- **Species:** rockfish, lingcod, salmon, surfperch, pacific-halibut, albacore-tuna, dungeness-crab
- **Wind window:** mornings only (Pacific wind builds by 11 AM consistently)

### Big Lagoon
- **Profile:** Brackish coastal lagoon, wind-only safety in default closed-spit state. Solo OK. `hasOceanFacingSpit: true`.
- **Spit advisory:** Always emitted in Safety layer when this launch is selected. When spit is open, the lagoon becomes a Tier-2 tidal bay (currents 3-6 kt through the breach, ocean swell wraps in, water level tracks ocean tide).
- **Species:** cutthroat (year-round at Big Lagoon — `perLaunchSeasonWindows` override), surfperch

### Stone Lagoon
- **Profile:** Same as Big Lagoon. Smaller water volume → breaches more reactively. `hasOceanFacingSpit: true`.
- **Special:** Cutthroat spawning closure Nov 21 – end of February (CDFW manages spit openings around fish-passage windows).
- **Species:** cutthroat (default Mar 1 – Nov 20 window), surfperch

### Mad River Slough
- **Profile:** Tidal but sheltered estuary. Wind + tide-awareness. Solo OK. No swell check.
- **Tidal currents:** HUB0203 station data drives the afternoon-slack launch window calculation.
- **Species:** surfperch, california-halibut, dungeness-crab

### Humboldt Bay (interior)
- **Profile:** Tidal bay channels (Samoa, Fields, Woodley, Eureka Public ramps). Solo OK away from entrance channel. **User personal rule: jetty crossing permanently ruled out, regardless of conditions.**
- **Tidal currents:** HUB0203 same as slough.
- **Species:** surfperch, california-halibut, dungeness-crab

### Freshwater Lagoon
- **Profile:** Closed-water freshwater lake. Wind-only safety. No tide. No spit (fed by stream, separate from the ocean-facing lagoons). Solo OK.
- **Species:** bluegill, largemouth-bass, rainbow-trout

### Permanently ruled out (documented in reference/launches.md but not in code)
- Humboldt Bay jetty entrance crossing — user-set permanent rule
- Outside Humboldt jetties (Trinidad is the only open-Pacific launch)
- Crescent City outside breakwater, Shelter Cove, Eel River past the mouth, Pedrazzini past the mouth — all Trinidad-only rule

## Species (12)

Each species has a `reference/regs/<species>-2026.md` reference file with verification TODOs, plus a runtime mirror in `src/lib/config/regs.ts`. All regs files include "TODO (verify before trusting)" sections — the user is responsible for confirming current-season specifics with CDFW before any trip.

### Open-Pacific species (Trinidad only)

**rockfish** — Rockfish (Northern Mgmt Area)
- Season: Apr 1 – Dec 31
- Bag: RCG aggregate 10/day; **vermilion + sunset sub-limit 4** in Northern Mgmt Area
- Required: CDFW Sport Fishing License, descender device (CCR T-14 §27.20(b)(2))
- **No retention:** bronzespotted, cowcod, quillback, yelloweye

**lingcod** — Lingcod (Northern Mgmt Area)
- Season: Apr 1 – Dec 31
- Bag: 2/day, possession 4
- Min size: 22 inches total length
- Required: descender device on board

**salmon** — Salmon (KMZ — Klamath Management Zone)
- Season: **Jun 13 – Jul 19 and Aug 1 – Aug 31** (CHECK every trip — CDFW can close inseason)
- **MUST call hotline 707-576-3429** within 2 hours of launch (`requiresHotlineVerify: true`)
- Min size: 20" (Chinook)
- Gear: max 2 single-point, single-shank, barbless hooks (NOT a circle-hook mandate)
- **No salmon report card required for ocean salmon** (report card is river-only)

**pacific-halibut** — Pacific Halibut
- Season: typical May 1 – Nov 15 with quota — **can close inseason** (`requiresHotlineVerify: true`, no phone but a "verify status" flag)
- Daily bag: 1, possession 2
- Min size: 22 inches
- **Required:** Pacific Halibut Card (free from CDFW, must record each kept fish)
- Trip notes: 3-5 mi offshore at Trinidad; 6-8 hour committed trip; not solo year 1

**albacore-tuna** — Albacore Tuna
- Season: year-round legal; realistic kayak season is Jun–Oct when water 60°F+ pushes inshore (most years albacore are 25-40 mi offshore = out of kayak range)
- No bag limit historically, no size limit
- **Future improvement:** SST (sea surface temperature) data source to gate viability automatically

**dungeness-crab** — Dungeness Crab (sport)
- Season: **Nov 1 – Jul 30** (typical sport window — sometimes delayed for whale entanglement risk)
- Daily bag: 10 crabs
- Min size: 5.75" across the back (widest part of carapace)
- Gear: max 10 hoop nets per person
- **Always check CDPH for domoic-acid closures** before launching
- Compatible launches: humboldt-bay-interior, mad-river-slough, trinidad (hoop net at the kelp edge)

### Bay / slough species

**surfperch** — Surfperch (Northern District)
- Season: year-round
- Daily bag: 20 fish total in surfperch family
- **Redtail surfperch sub-limit** applies (verify with CDFW; was historically 10/day)
- No size limit on most surfperch
- Compatible launches: trinidad (beach), mad-river-slough, humboldt-bay-interior, big-lagoon, stone-lagoon

**california-halibut** — California Halibut (Northern District)
- Season: year-round in CA marine waters
- Daily bag: 3 fish, possession 6
- Min size: 22 inches total length
- Compatible launches: humboldt-bay-interior, mad-river-slough

### Brackish-lagoon species

**cutthroat** — Coastal Cutthroat Trout (Humboldt Lagoons)
- **Launch-aware season** via `perLaunchSeasonWindows`:
  - Big Lagoon: year-round (Jan 1 – Dec 31)
  - Stone Lagoon: Mar 1 – Nov 20 (Nov 21 – end of Feb spawning closure)
- Daily bag: 1 fish (both lagoons historically)
- Gear: artificial-lure only, single-point barbless hook (historically — verify 2026)
- Catch-and-release encouraged (species of conservation concern)

### Freshwater Lagoon species

**bluegill** — Bluegill (CA Inland)
- Season: year-round
- No bag limit, no size limit in most CA inland waters

**largemouth-bass** — Largemouth Bass
- Season: year-round
- Daily bag: 5 (combined largemouth/smallmouth/spotted)
- Min size: 12 inches

**rainbow-trout** — Rainbow Trout (stocker)
- Season: year-round (statewide inland)
- Daily bag: 5
- **Plant-schedule dependent** — best fishing 1-2 days after a CDFW plant; check the stocking schedule
