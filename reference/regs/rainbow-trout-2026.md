# Rainbow Trout (Stocker / Inland) — California 2026

**Last updated:** 2026-05-17 (DRAFT — verify with CDFW before relying on)
**Region:** California freshwater inland waters; here, planted rainbow trout in Freshwater Lagoon
**Sources for verification:**

## Season

**Open year-round** in most California inland waters (including Freshwater Lagoon). Some streams have season restrictions; lakes/lagoons typically year-round.

This species refers to **stocked rainbow trout** in Freshwater Lagoon — distinct from coastal cutthroat trout in Big Lagoon / Stone Lagoon (those are wild populations under separate, stricter regs — see `cutthroat-2026.md`).

## Bag limits

- **Daily bag:** 5 trout (general statewide inland waters)
- **Possession:** 10 trout (2× daily)

Some specific waters have lower limits or catch-and-release zones — Freshwater Lagoon historically follows the statewide 5-fish limit but verify CDFW page before trip.

## Size limits

- **None statewide** for inland trout in most lakes
- Some Wild Trout waters have minimum size + gear restrictions; verify per-water

## License and stamps

- **Required:** CDFW Sport Fishing License (age 16+)
- **No special trout stamp** required for inland waters
- **No salmon report card** (that's anadromous-only)

## Gear and technique

- Light spinning rod, 4-6 lb test
- PowerBait, garlic-scented dough, or salmon eggs on small hooks (size 12-16)
- Small spinners (#0-#2 Mepps, Roostertails) and inline spoons
- Drift-fishing from a kayak with light split shot
- **Barbless hooks recommended** for catch-and-release — required at some special-regs waters; not statewide for stocker trout but good practice

## Stocking schedule

CDFW plants rainbow trout in Freshwater Lagoon on a periodic schedule. **Check the CDFW fish-planting schedule** for the current week's plants — productive fishing is best 1-2 days after a plant.

## Launch suitability

- **Freshwater Lagoon** — primary stocker-trout target
- Not the cutthroat / Big Lagoon / Stone Lagoon target (different species, different regs — see `cutthroat-2026.md`)
