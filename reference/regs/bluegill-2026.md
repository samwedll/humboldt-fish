# Bluegill — California Inland Waters 2026

**Last updated:** 2026-05-17 (DRAFT — verify with CDFW before relying on)
**Region:** California freshwater; specifically the user's Humboldt-area inland lakes
**Source for verification:** <https://wildlife.ca.gov/Fishing/Inland>

---

## Season

**Open year-round** in California inland waters for bluegill (and other sunfish).

## Bag limits

- **Daily bag:** No limit on bluegill / sunfish in most California inland waters (verify per-water for specific lakes)
- **Possession:** Same — no statewide limit

## Size limits

- **None statewide** for bluegill

## License and stamps

- **Required:** CDFW Sport Fishing License (age 16+)
- **No ocean enhancement stamp** required (inland water)
- **No special inland validations** for bluegill in most waters

## Gear and technique

- Light spinning rod with 4-6 lb test
- Small hooks (size 8-12) with worms, mealworms, or wax worms
- Small jigs (1/64-1/32 oz), small spinners, or fly-rod poppers
- Bobber rigs work well from shore or kayak; sight-fishing the bed in spring

## Launch suitability

- **Freshwater Lagoon** — primary target water (Tier 1 protected lagoon, closed water)
- **Big Lagoon / Stone Lagoon** — bluegill present but cutthroat trout is the primary draw
- Not present in saltwater launches
